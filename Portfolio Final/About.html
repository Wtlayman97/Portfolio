I have taken on an expedited courseload to finish my backelors of science in just two years and am working toward my career in full-stack development. Through some personal projects and pro-bono work, I am working to expand my knowledge in Angular, React, Ruby, and GoLang to better complement my proficiencies in Java, Python, PHP, JavaScript, and HTML.
